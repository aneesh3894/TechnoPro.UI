    About Us

    TechnoPro Contracting Services W.L.L. provides high-quality contracting,  Engineering, trading, and all related support  Services to all clients. The company is controlled by a management team with a clear  focus on quality and safety in its field of operations, and also with a high level expertise. Our qualified and experienced personnel effectively  manage the estimation, engineering planning, management, construction, safety, quality control/quality assurance, and cost control  of projects. We are esteemed ourselves, being able to offer specialized areas of  construction, including civil, mechanical, and piping, insulation, environmental and various other areas. In  addition to our highly qualified workforce, we also have a considerable  fleet of construction equipment. Our head office is located in East riffa , Bahrain and also we have our  Operations all over the Kingdom of Bahrain. The operations of the company and  scope of activities have ever being expanding and diversifying. Workshops, Yards, Warehouses,  and Employee Accommodation Facilities are under formation at various places in the  Kingdom of Bahrain. We look forward to have a long standing business relation with  our esteemed customers with our sincere services.
